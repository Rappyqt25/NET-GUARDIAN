EXAMPLE OF CYBERCRIME

PHISHING SITE

A phishing site is a fraudulent website designed to trick users into providing sensitive information, such as passwords or credit card details, by impersonating a legitimate website or company.

SOFTWARE PIRACY

Software piracy is the unauthorized use or distribution of copyrighted software, which is illegal and can result in penalties or legal action

MALWARE

Malware is a type of software that is intentionally designed to cause harm to computers, networks, and mobile devices

RANSOMEWARE

Ransomware is a type of malicious software that encrypts a victim's files or locks them out of their device, demanding payment in exchange for the decryption key or access to the device.

IOT(INTERNET OF THINGS)

IoT (Internet of Things) devices can be vulnerable to cybercrime, as they may lack adequate security measures and can be exploited by attackers to gain access to networks, steal data, or launch DDoS attacks.

IDENTITY THEFT

Identity theft is a type of crime where an individual's personal or financial information is stolen and used by someone else for fraudulent purposes, such as making unauthorized purchases, opening new accounts, or obtaining loans..
